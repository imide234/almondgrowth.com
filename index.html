Our Story

IVD MARKETING ENTERPRISE was founded in 2021 by Imide Victor with a vision to help businesses move beyond shallow marketing and build lasting customer relationships through digital systems.

While many businesses focus only on getting attention, IVD MARKETING ENTERPRISE believes the real power of marketing is customer retention, trust, and long-term loyalty.

We saw that many small businesses struggle with inconsistent sales, disappearing customers, and weak customer engagement after the first purchase. Instead of simply helping businesses attract customers, we focus on helping them keep customers.

Through CRM (Customer Relationship Management) systems, digital marketing strategies, and evolving AI-powered solutions, IVD MARKETING ENTERPRISE built systems designed to help businesses create repeat revenue, stronger customer relationships, and sustainable growth.

In 2026, Imide Victor and David transitioned IVD MARKETING ENTERPRISE into Almond Growth — a renewed mission to solve the real growth problem for small businesses: not just getting customers, but keeping them.

Almond Growth exists to help businesses grow their sales, boost revenue, and retain customers using the power of CRM and strategy. We empower businesses to reach their ideal customers confidently and sustain growth without guesswork.
