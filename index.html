Our Story

Built to Solve the Problem Most Agencies Ignore.

Thousands of agencies help businesses get attention. Almost none focus on what happens after.

IVD Marketing Enterprise was founded in 2021 by Imide Victor with a conviction that most small businesses don't need more marketing — they need better customer relationships.

Working with businesses across Nigeria, we saw a consistent pattern: companies investing heavily in attracting new customers while ignoring the gold sitting in their existing customer base. Customers were coming in — but not coming back.

We built systems to fix that. CRM pipelines that tracked real relationships. WhatsApp funnels that re-engaged dormant buyers. Lifecycle campaigns that created genuine loyalty. And the results were undeniable.

In 2026, Imide Victor and David transitioned IVD Marketing Enterprise into Almond Growth — a focused, specialist CRM and customer retention growth company with one clear mission:

Help small businesses stop losing customers — and turn the ones they have into lifetime, loyal revenue.

We are not a general marketing agency. We are not an AI automation tool. We are a customer retention growth company — and in Nigeria, in Africa, and across small business markets globally, that focus is our greatest strength.
